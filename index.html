Our Story

The taste of Nepal,
rooted in Kirtipur

Born from a family love of feeding people well, The Kirtipur Restro serves the food this valley actually craves — sekuwa charred over open flame, sukuti and chilly tossed in a screaming-hot wok, khana sets that fill the table, and tandoori favourites from the clay oven. Every marinade is made in-house, and every guest is family.

From flame-licked skewers to a slow evening over hookah and a cold Gorkha — we do it the unhurried way, because that's the only way it should taste.

01.The Marinade

Timur, ginger, garlic and home-ground masala — every sekuwa and chilly starts hours before it ever meets the fire.

02.The Flame

Skewers over open coals, the wok at full heat, and the clay tandoor breathing life into naan and tikka through the day.

03.The Table

Warm lighting, warmer hospitality. A place for families, celebrations, and long evenings with friends alike.

04.The Promise

Honest recipes. Local ingredients. The true taste of Nepal, every time you visit.
